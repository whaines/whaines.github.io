My design principles and values for teams
I strongly believe design needs to push beyond the visuals, beyond the experience, and beyond the screen. When done properly, design can be a force for change, both in a business and in a person’s day to day life.
  • 1
    Design businesses, not (just) screens
  • 2
    Use qual and quant data to inform design
  • 3
    Leverage design to find product growth areas
  • 4
    Work upstream with partners, together
  • 5
    Balance user and business success when possible
  • 6
    Be a force for inclusivity and change
