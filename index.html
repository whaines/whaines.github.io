Hey there, I’m Wes
Nice to Meet You
I design and build digital products using strategy, visuals and code. My work meets at the intersection of product and service design. I design with data, work cross-functionally, and advocate for user research.

I believe design needs to push beyond the visuals, beyond the experience, and beyond the screen. When done properly, design can be a positive force for change, both in a business and in a person’s day to day life.
